# Product

## Register

brand

## Users
**Primary**: Individuals looking for custom portraits, premium framing, gift artworks, home decoration, and luxury wall art.
**Secondary**: Corporate clients seeking office art installations, reception artworks, and corporate framing projects.
**Tertiary**: Interior designers, architects, real estate developers, art collectors, and luxury homeowners.

## Product Purpose
To serve as a world-class digital experience that positions Darlington Wosa Art & Frames Ltd as the leading premium art and framing brand in Nigeria and beyond. It functions as an immersive digital art gallery, luxury creative studio, and premium customer experience platform that converts visitors into paying customers, enables service bookings, and manages projects and referrals seamlessly.

## Brand Personality
Premium, creative, and futuristic luxury. The tone is artistic, elegant, and professional. It should evoke the feeling of entering a high-end luxury art gallery or an artist's creative workspace with Apple-level attention to detail. 

## Anti-references
- Not a static or typical business website.
- Not a traditional corporate website.
- Not a template.
- Avoid gimmicky effects; motion must be purposeful and premium.

## Design Principles
1. **Artistic Storytelling Through Motion**: Art tools and elements (paint brushes, charcoal dust, frames) should animate and become part of the narrative to guide the user naturally.
2. **Immersive Exhibition**: Every section must feel handcrafted and gallery-like, blending luxury presentation with depth-based scrolling.
3. **Showcase Excellence**: Let the artwork speak for itself with a museum-grade presentation that immediately builds trust and communicates professionalism.
4. **Seamless Conversion**: While functioning as a luxury gallery, the platform must intuitively drive lead generation, bookings, and customer account management without breaking the premium immersion.

## Accessibility & Inclusion
Ensure broad accessibility alongside high-end motion design. Includes robust mobile optimization, clear semantic structure, and accessible alternative text for artworks. Motion should respect `prefers-reduced-motion` settings where appropriate so that the site remains usable for all users without losing its premium feel.
