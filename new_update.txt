For the paint and sip event booking there are some questions or form they will fill when booking online


1. Wheres the venue
2. Is it an indoor event or an outdoor event
3. What day are you looking at?
4. Whats your audience age group?

5. How many hours is the painting session planned to last?
6. Who is responsible for providing the tables and chairs for the participants
7. What is the backup plan in case of poor weather at the outdoor venue?
8. Will there be direct access to water and electricity at the outdoor site?
9. Will the you handle the refreshments, or are we expected to provide them?
10. What size of canvas is expected for the session? The standard size is 10*12 or 12*16
11. Are there any additional take-home items required beyond the standard canvas?
12. How much time will I have at the venue to set up and pack up your materials?

All these questions are required to ensure clients get an accurate quote that covers all logistics—especially if its an outdoor setup
